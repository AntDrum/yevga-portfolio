About me

I’m Yevheniia, a professional retoucher with over 5 years of experience in high-end e-commerce, jewelry, beauty, and fashion retouching. I specialize in clean, commercial visuals with precise skin, product, fabric, and color correction for web and print.

I’ve collaborated with international photographers, fashion brands, and marketing agencies, including Helen Marlen Group, Terani Couture (NYC), Upscale-Marketing (EU), She's Waisted (USA), Dominique Renée® (USA), Ellie Lowe(GB), Snap Supplements (USA), Scavini and Véronique Leroy (France), and startups like Hilu (USA). My work includes e-commerce, headshots, interiors, and campaign imagery, as well as collaborations with fashion photographer Marty Gottlieb, with publications in Marika Magazine and displays in Times Square, New York. I’ve also retouched campaigns featuring models such as Maria Moda, Megan Victoria Hanks, and Lynn Kelly.
